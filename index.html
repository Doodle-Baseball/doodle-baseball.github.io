    How to Play Doodle Baseball

    Mastering Doodle Baseball is easy with these simple steps:

    1. Start the game: Visit Doodle Baseball and click to begin
    2. Batting: Click or tap when the ball crosses the strike zone to hit
    3. Timing: Perfect your swing timing for home runs
    4. Scoring: Earn points for hits and runs
    5. Advance: Progress through innings to face tougher pitches
