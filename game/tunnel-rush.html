    How to Play Tunnel Rush

    The concept of Tunnel Rush is straightforward but mastering it requires skill and quick reflexes. Here's how to play:

    Basic Controls

    • Keyboard: Use the Left and Right Arrow keys to move side to side
    • Mobile: Simply tap the left or right side of the screen to move
    • Spacebar: Pause the game (in some versions)

    Gameplay Mechanics

    1. Speed increases gradually as you progress through the tunnel
    2. Avoid obstacles by moving left or right through the openings
    3. Watch for color changes - some versions feature obstacles that match the tunnel color
    4. Survive as long as possible - the game ends when you hit an obstacle
    5. Compete for high scores - try to beat your personal best distance

    Tips and Strategies for Tunnel Rush

    Want to improve your Tunnel Rush skills? Try these expert tips:

    1. Focus Ahead

    Don't look at your character - focus several obstacles ahead to anticipate movements.

    2. Use Peripheral Vision

    Train yourself to see the entire screen, not just the center.

    3. Stay Centered

    When possible, remain in the middle to give yourself maximum reaction time.

    4. Practice Pattern Recognition

    Many obstacles follow repeating patterns - learn to recognize them.

    5. Stay Calm

    Panic leads to mistakes. Breathe steadily and maintain focus.
