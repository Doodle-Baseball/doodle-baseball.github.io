    OvO Game – Test Your Arcade Skills

    Jump, smash, dive, and slide in OvO! Guide your stickman through challenging levels, avoiding falls and reaching the final flag. Each level presents unique obstacles, and a timer challenges you to beat your best time.

    Game Features

    • Navigate through 9 sections with 61 levels.
    • Master diving, smashing, and sliding to overcome obstacles.
    • Choose from various skins, including the popular "MaterWelon OVO".
    • Test both your parkour and puzzle-solving skills.

    How to Play

    The game provides a tutorial in early levels, but here are key controls:

    • Diving: Jump and press the down arrow to cover longer gaps.
    • Smashing: Jump and press down to break checked floors.
    • Sliding: Press down while running to pass under obstacles.

    Tips for Difficult Levels

    • Analyze paths carefully to find the safest route.
    • Fast-paced gameplay requires practice—keep trying!
    • Check walkthroughs if you're stuck on a tricky level.

    Controls

    • Arrow Keys – Move
    • Spacebar – Jump
    • R – Restart Level
