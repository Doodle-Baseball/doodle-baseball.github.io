    Getting started with Drive Mad 2 is simple, but mastering the game requires skill and precision. Here's what you need to know:

    Basic Controls

    • Spacebar: Handbrake for sharp turns and drifts
    • R Key: Reset your vehicle if you get stuck
    • Shift Key: Boost (when available)

    Gameplay Mechanics

    Drive Mad 2 features:

    1. Challenging Tracks: Navigate through obstacle courses filled with ramps, loops, and precarious platforms
    2. Vehicle Physics: Realistic physics that make every jump and landing feel impactful
    3. Multiple Vehicles: Unlock and choose from different cars, trucks, and special vehicles
    4. Progression System: Complete levels to unlock new challenges and vehicles
    Pro Tip: Master the handbrake turns early - they're essential for navigating the game's sharp corners and tricky obstacles!

    Advanced Strategies for Drive Mad 2

    To truly master Drive Mad 2, you'll need more than just basic driving skills. Here are some advanced techniques:

    1. Perfecting Your Landings

    Successful jumps require:

    • Approaching ramps at the right speed
    • Adjusting your vehicle's angle mid-air
    • Landing on all four wheels when possible

    2. Managing Momentum

    The key to many levels is maintaining just enough speed to complete obstacles without losing control.

    3. Learning From Failures
