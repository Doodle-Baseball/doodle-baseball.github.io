    Burger Bounty – Run Your Own Burger Joint

    Experience the thrill of managing a burger restaurant in Burger Bounty! Start as the sole worker and gradually expand by adding tables, food, and staff. Serve customers quickly to earn money and grow your business.

    Game Features

    • Unlock and upgrade stations to improve efficiency.
    • Hire waiters and expand your restaurant.
    • Use a hoverboard to deliver orders and clean tables faster.
    • Serve demanding "star customers" for bigger rewards.

    How to Play

    On Desktop: Use arrow keys or W, A, S, D to move.

    On Mobile: Drag and move to play.

    Customization

    Visit an appearance station to change your character’s outfit and gender.

    Unlocking Features

    • Hoverboard: Unlocks after the 3rd table.
    • More Helpers: Unlock by earning more money and adding tables.
