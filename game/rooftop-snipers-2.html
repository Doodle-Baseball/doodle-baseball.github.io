    Rooftop Snipers: The Ultimate 2-Player Dueling Game

    Rooftop Snipers is one of the most entertaining and addictive 2-player shooting games available online. With its simple controls, hilarious physics, and competitive gameplay, it's perfect for quick gaming sessions with friends. Whether you're looking for a fun way to pass time or a competitive duel, Rooftop Snipers delivers endless entertainment.

    How to Play Rooftop Snipers

    The premise of Rooftop Snipers is simple but incredibly fun. Two players face off on opposite sides of a rooftop, armed with sniper rifles. The goal is to knock your opponent off the rooftop while maintaining your own balance.

    Basic Controls:

    • Player 1: Use W (jump), A (move left), D (move right), and S (shoot)
    • Player 2: Use arrow keys (↑ to jump, ← and → to move, ↓ to shoot)

    Game Mechanics:

    1. Recoil Physics: Every shot you fire pushes you backward due to recoil
    2. Knockback Effect: Hitting your opponent pushes them toward the edge
    3. One-Hit KOs: A single successful shot knocks out your opponent
    4. First to 5 Wins: Matches are best of 5 rounds

    Key Features of Rooftop Snipers

    1. Simple Yet Deep Gameplay

    While the controls are easy to learn, mastering Rooftop Snipers requires skill. You'll need to time your shots perfectly, manage recoil, and predict your opponent's movements.

    2. Hilarious Physics System

    The exaggerated physics make every match unpredictable and funny. Watching characters flail as they're knocked off buildings never gets old.

    3. Multiple Game Modes

    Beyond the standard duel mode, many versions of Rooftop Snipers offer:

    • Tournament mode
    • Challenge mode against AI
    • Custom matches with special rules

    4. Character Selection

    Choose from various quirky characters, each with slightly different physics properties that affect gameplay.

    5. Perfect for Local Multiplayer

    The game shines when played with a friend on the same keyboard, making it ideal for parties or casual gaming sessions.

    Advanced Strategies for Rooftop Snipers

    Pro Tips to Dominate Your Opponent:

    • Recoil Jumping: Time your shots to use recoil for extra jump height
    • Edge Guarding: Position yourself to knock opponents off when they respawn
    • Fake Outs: Pretend to shoot to bait your opponent into moving
    • Angle Shots: Shoot while moving to change your trajectory
    • Patience: Sometimes waiting for your opponent to make the first move pays off

    Why Rooftop Snipers is So Addictive

    Several factors contribute to Rooftop Snipers' lasting appeal:

    1. Instant Gratification
